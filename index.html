Who We Are

White Lily tattoo studio is Rupert Burton sSequeira's baby famous for its fully customised tattoos.

Rupert has been tattooing since 2010, during which he had the opportunity to work and get acquainted with some of the best tattoo artists not only in Mumbai and across India, but also internationally like New Zealand. An ex-character animator for Disney turned tattoo artist, he has always been artistic since childhood. Meeting wonderful people and creating something unique for them has been an extension of his love for art and creativity.

Customising tattoos is his way of creating something unique and personal for his clients, which make the whole process meaningful for them. Cover-ups/modification is his forte, which is proven by his work and client testimonials. The challenges, the stories and the priceless look on the clients face after the tattoo, is what excites him about the whole process of cover up tattoos.

"You're never done learning…."

this mantra keeps him grounded as a person/artist.

FAQ

We aim to provide the best experience of using our tattoo services. We hope these frequently asked questions and their answers will help you find a solution to your problem. If not, feel free to contact us anytime.

How do I schedule an appointment?

We ask that you call for more information on scheduling. White Lily Tattoo Studio: +91-9820592398 or fill in the above form.

How do I prepare myself before a tattoo?

Eating before getting a tattoo is necessary, as tattooing can be a lengthy process. Carry energy drinks or chocolates in order to maintain sugar levels and avoid dehydration. Resting well prior to getting a tattoo is very important.

Does a tattoo hurt?

Yes, there is pain involved with the tattoo process. The pain is only temporary & far from unbearable. We recommend eating before coming in for your tattoo. A little bit of carbohydrates will help.

How much does a tattoo cost?

Tattoo pricing is subjective. The best way to get an estimate for your tattoo is to stop in at White Lily Tattoo Studio. Bring any ideas or reference images you have. We want to hear & see your ideas. We can work out the BEST deal for you.

Can you tell me a price over the phone?

Sure. We could tell you any price. The truth is, we probably wouldn’t even get in the ballpark of what your tattoo would cost. Asking how much a tattoo costs without providing all of the correct information is like asking how much a bag of groceries cost. It really depends on what’s in the bag. There are so many variables in the tattoo process; design, placement, size, detail. All of these factors determine the price of a tattoo. The best way to get information is to stop in & go through your ideas with a member of our staff. Everyone at White Lily Tattoo Studio is knowledgeable about the process & can help you get the process started for your new tattoo.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Appointments are always best, but sometimes tattoos can be spur of the moment. We would love to accommodate you. Stop in or call for availability.

Can I bring my own image?

Yes, we love to see your ideas as a reference. We understand not everyone is an artist, but any help you can offer us to get the ideas out of your head & onto paper would be amazing.

Can you fix my old tattoo?

Yes. The tattoo industry has come a very long way in a short period of time. We would love to breathe life back into your tattoos.

Can you cover up an old tattoo?

That’s what we Specialize in. But, sometimes not all tattoos can be covered. Stop in & show it to us. We can tell you whether it will be possible.

Will my tattoo fade?

Maybe. We give you all the proper knowledge on how to prevent fading. If you follow our directions, it will minimize wear & tear to prevent this from happening. A proper sunblock should always be placed over a tattoo when exposed to the sun. 10-15 years after the tattoo, it may show some wear & fading, however, the skin you have without tattoos will also look differently in 10-15 years as your body ages.

Do you tattoo minors?

Only with parental consent. A parent or guardian will have to sit through the whole process.
