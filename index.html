About me

White Lily tattoo studio is Rupert Burton Sequeira's baby famous for its fully customised tattoos.

Who We Are

White Lily Tattoo Studio is a private studio that specializes in exceptional coverups and offers a unique and enjoyable experience to create fully customized tattoos that reflect your style and preferences. White Lily is Rupert’s baby, an accomplished tattoo artist who has had the privilege of working with some of the best in the industry, both in India and overseas, since 2010. With his expertise and our commitment to delivering personalized and high-quality service, you are bound to have a memorable experience that you'll cherish for years to come.

It’s all about the expression

Whether it’s a tattoo for tribute, reminder, aesthetics, coverup, body mods… it’s all a personal and emotional journey of EXPRESSION. And he is here to help you! With a 100% SAFE, sterile environment & equipment.

Our Services

We provide a wide variety of tattooing services to both regular and new clients. At White Lily, you can expect first-class treatment as well as 100% safe and sterile environment & equipment.
